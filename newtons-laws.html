Newton's Laws




Law One

What is Newton's First Law?

Newton's first law (the law of interia) is stated as "An object at rest stays at rest and an object in motion
stays in motion with the same speed and in the same direction unless acted upon by a unbalanced force".


Newton's First Law in Untamed

Newton's first law is crutial to this roller coasters and most roller coasters that aren't always powered by a motor. In Untamed this law doesn't
come into play until after the vertical climb. This is due to the fact that the car is moved
up the vertical climb using a motor. Due to the fact that the car is in motion thanks
to the motor before it goes down the vertical climb, it stays in
motion throughout the rest of the ride. Over time forces such as gravity and friction slow the car down.









Law Two

What is Newton's Second Law?

Newton's second law is stated as "The acceleration of an object as produced by a net force is directly proportional
to the magnitude of the net force, in the same direction as the net force, and inversely proportional to the mass of the object".


Newton's Second Law in Untamed

Even though it might not be a very good example, it still works. The speed of the car going down the track, if affected by the weight of the cart.
This means that is there is no one in the car it will go slower than if someone was in the cart. This might not be a problem with the
design of this coaster but if the speed wasn't fast enough (due to the car not being heavy enough) than it might not be able to
go through the loops, makeing the car stuck in the middle of the ride.









Law Three

What is Newton's Third Law?

Newton's third law is stated as "For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction".


Newton's Third Law in Untamed

An example of Newton's third law on Untamed has to do with the seat. A passenger pushes on the
seat of the roller coaster and the seat pushes him/her back wiht an equal and opposite force.
