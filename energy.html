Kinetic

What is Kinetic Energy?

Kinetic energy is the energy that is produced when an object is in motion.
Kinetic energy is measured in joules and the formula is K=0.5mv2. (m=mass & v=speed)


Kinetic Energy for Untamed

For most of Untamed you are moving. During that time you have always changing amount of kinetic energy.
Inorder to be able to calculate the kinetic energy, we need to get two things. First we need the speed and the weight of the
vehicle's weight. From previous calculations, we know the the speed is 44mph at its max and from our sources, we know that
the weight of the vehicle is 435kg. We can now use the formula for kinetic energy (K=0.5mv2) to get the max kinetic energy
for Untamed. Untamed's kinetic energy is:

K = 421,080J


Work for Kinetic Energy Result

In order to figure out the Kinetic energy, we need to use the formula: K=0.5mv2
Steps:
1. K = 0.5 x 435 x 442
2. K = 0.5 x 435 x 1,936
3. K = 0.5 x 842,160
4. K = 421,080J

What is Potential Energy?

Potential energy is the energy an object prossesses by virtue of its position in a feild. Two examples of such feilds
are gravitational and electrical. The formula for potential energy is P.E.=mxgxh (m=mass in kg, g=gravitational acceleration,
h=height)and it is measured in joules (J).


Potential Energy for Untamed

Even though most of the ride is moving, there are some brief moments when the ride stops (or comes close to stopping).
One big example of this is when the ride is at the top of the vertial climb right before it goes down at 97 degrees. From collected data, we know
that the car's weight is 435kg and that the height is 72ft at that point. So when you put this into the formula, you get the following potential energy:

PE = 307,144J


Work for Kinetic Energy Result

In order to figure out the Potential energy, we need to use the formula: P.E.=mxgxh
Steps:
1. PE = 435 x 1 x 72
2. PE = 435 x 72
3. PE = 307,144J
